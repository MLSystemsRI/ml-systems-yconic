Everything They've Tried

14 years of attempts. None have worked.

2012
Station opens. WJ Management LLC (Chicago) hired to operate. Ridership immediately below projections.
2012–2014
Free fares offered to attract riders. Still can't fill the garage. MBTA spends $10M, collects $3.68M.
2013
RIDOT proposes selling adjacent park-and-ride lot to CVS for $1.9M, redirecting users to the empty garage. Tabled by State Properties Committee.
2015
RIDOT takes over operations from WJ Management to save $340K/year. Director Alviti: "reducing the cost of operations."
2016–2020
Town Council votes 3-2 for TOD (Transit-Oriented Development) zoning to attract density. No sewer service, no connectivity. Nothing built.
2020–2024
COVID collapses remaining ridership. Floors 3 and 4 permanently closed. 157 daily riders. Trend worsening.
